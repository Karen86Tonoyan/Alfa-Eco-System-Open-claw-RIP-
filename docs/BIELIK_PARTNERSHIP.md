# ALFA × Bielik — Partnership Brief

## Why this document exists

This note exists as a public-facing partnership direction, not as a disclosure of the private operational core.
It explains why the ALFA control layer is relevant for a Polish-language model ecosystem such as Bielik.

## Strategic fit

- Polish AI safety needs Polish context awareness.
- `Filtry Tonoyana` were built around linguistic, behavioral, and cultural patterns that standard safety layers often miss.
- A domestic model gains strategic value when safety and orchestration are also sovereign.

## What ALFA can contribute

- `Filters`
  A structured anti-hallucination and verification layer.
- `Cerber`
  An execution gate that decides before action.
- `Guardian lineage`
  Monitoring and anomaly-oriented safety thinking.
- `TDCM`
  A methodological frame for safer model behavior design.
- `Public proof repo`
  A visible, inspectable control plane that demonstrates architectural seriousness.

## Public vs private boundary

This repository is enough to evaluate the quality of the public control layer.
It is not a disclosure of:

- the private Cerber core,
- protected policy thresholds,
- hidden connector logic,
- commercial deployment internals.

## Collaboration modes

- research evaluation,
- safety integration pilot,
- B2B partnership for selected protected modules.
